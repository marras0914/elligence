SAP ERP Human Capital Management (HCM)

The best businesses in the world “run simple” with the use of SAP software to handle their day-to-day business transactions and processes. SAP ERP HCM is a comprehensive, integrated human resources management solution that delivers unmatched global capability. SAP HCM gives organizations like yours the tools needed to manage your organization’s most important asset, your employees.

Enhancing the SAP ERP HCM User Experience

Modernization tools like SAP Fiori UX and HR Renewal are dramatically enhancing core functions in SAP HCM with their intuitive design, while positively impacting business value. SAP Fiori UX offers a more personalized, responsive, and simple user experience for real-time business transactions, while HR Renewal allows HR to perform the same tasks as they did utilizing SAP ERP HCM screens, but with far fewer clicks.
