Seamless Hybrid Solutions

The future of HCM is in the cloud, but your organization’s journey to the cloud is unique, and most importantly, at your own pace. SAP offers a diverse portfolio of HCM applications that can be deployed on-premise, in the cloud, or through a combination, commonly referred to as the hybrid approach. With our extensive on-premise experience, we realize that customers need to recognize maximum return from existing investments. Our approach is to assess your objectives and existing landscape to guide you in your HCM journey to the cloud, leveraging the hybrid approach when appropriate along the way.
